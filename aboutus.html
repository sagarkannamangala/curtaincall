ABOUT CURTAINCALLINC

Founded in 1990 in association with The Stamford Community Arts Council, Curtain Call moved to our present location in the Sterling Farms Theatre Complex in 1992 -- in cooperation with the City of Stamford. Our Offices and theatres are located on the Sterling Farms Theatre Complex at 1349 Newfield Avenue, Stamford, CT 06905. Business Office Hours are from 10:00 to 6:00 Monday through Friday. Our Box Office hours are Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday, 10am - 4pm. Performance Fridays and Saturdays, 3pm - 9pm, and performance Sundays 10am - 5pm.


Curtain Call is Stamford's longest-running, and only nonprofit producing theatre company. With year-round performances in The Kweskin Theatre and The Dressing Room Theatre - conveniently located at Sterling Farms in Stamford - we also produce an annual free outdoor Shakespeare production. With at least 12 full-scale productions each year, plus many other special events (Interactive Murder Mysteries, Comedy Nights, Concerts, etc.) we entertain more than 25,000 patrons annually. Additionally, we offer a full curriculum of performing arts programming for ages five through adult.

Get involved with Curtain Call and learn why for eight years running, we have been named Fairfield County's Best Local Theatre Group. Also, in a state-wide FaceBook poll administered by the CT Tourism Board in August 2012, Curtain Call was the State's Favorite Theatre Site. In 2011 Curtain Call received the prestigious GOVERNOR'S AWARD FOR EXCELLENCE IN CULTURE AND TOURISM.

Support from individuals, corporations, small businesses and foundations help support our operation. Sponsorships and program advertising also help underwrite our various operating costs. For more information, please call 203-329-8207 x11 or write info@curtaincallinc.com For a brief video about who we are, visit this page.


Mission Statement

The goal of Curtain Call, Inc. is to provide an outlet for artistic expression for lower Fairfield County while entertaining the general public and to provide a safe and nurturing environment for learning.
