Ettiquette

Please turn off cell phones and chiming watches while inside the theatre. Texting is not allowed inside the theater: the light emitted is distracting to both performers and other audience members. Due to these distractions, we ask that you refrain from reading and writing text messages or e-mails, checking social media, playing games or otherwise using phones, tablet computers and other portable electronic devices during the performance. After the show, feel free to call/text/e-mail/tweet/update your friends about the great theater you just saw at Curtain Call!

We support Connecticut-based non-profit organizations, with priority given to producing arts organizations, public and private schools, health-related services for individuals, and community organizations in the Fairfield County area.
Due to the overwhelming demand, we are unable to honor donation requests for organizations located outside of Connecticut at this time.
All ticket donations are made at the sole discretion of Curtain Call based on the requesting organization and the services provided by the organization. Most often, we will provide two ticket vouchers each good for one ticket to a performance at Curtain Call.
** (Restrictions: All shows are subject to availability. Vouchers cannot be redeemed for cash or applied toward the cost of a subscription.)**
Occasionally, we will donate Flex Passes which are good for 5 to 10 tickets.

Photography

The taking of photographs or recordings of any kind before, during or after the performance without written permission from Curtain Call is strictly prohibited.

Smoking

Smoking is prohibited everywhere in our theatres.

Lists

We do not sell or share our mailing lists. We do, however, often purchase lists from outside mail houses, so sometimes those who have previously requested no mail from us, do receive communication. We apologize for that potential inconvenience.
