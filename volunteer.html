As Fairfield County's most active community theatre organization, we are always welcoming new volunteers to our family. Keeping two venues (TheKweskin Theatre and The Dressing Room Theatre) hopping with year-round plays, musicals, acting classes and much more, takes hundreds of volunteer hours.

While we always need help from volunteer performers, technicians etc. for our productions, we are currently looking for help in our Box Office. Box Office volunteers take reservations during the week and also help out on performance days. One day a week, or once a month can be a great help. Computer skills are helpful but not mandatory.

Other volunteer opportunities include ushering, set building, painting,costume help, public relations and marketing, to name a few. If you'd like more information on volunteer opportunities, click here.
